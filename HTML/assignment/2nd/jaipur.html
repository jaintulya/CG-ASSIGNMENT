Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is famously known as the ‘Pink City’ for its unique pink-colored buildings and royal charm. Rich in history and culture, Jaipur is part of India’s famous Golden Triangle (Delhi, Agra, Jaipur) and attracts travelers from across the world.

The city is home to magnificent palaces, forts, and heritage sites like Hawa Mahal, Amer Fort, City Palace, and Jantar Mantar (a UNESCO World Heritage site). Its bustling bazaars are filled with handicrafts, jewelry, textiles, and traditional Rajasthani art, making it a shopper’s paradise.
